Calisthenics Progression Table

Level Exercise Reps/Sets Description/Goal
Beginner Push-Ups 3 sets of 5-10 reps Focus on form, building strength in the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Goal: Achieving consistent push-ups with proper form.
Beginner Bodyweight Squats 3 sets of 10-15 reps Building lower body strength and mobility. Focus on form and depth, ensuring knees don't go beyond toes.
Beginner Plank 3 sets of 20-30 seconds Core stabilization. Focus on maintaining a straight line from head to heels.
Intermediate Push-Ups 4 sets of 12-20 reps Begin incorporating variations like knee push-ups or incline push-ups if needed. Work on increasing endurance and form.
Intermediate Lunges 4 sets of 10-12 reps per leg Focus on balance, strength, and endurance in the lower body. Progress to weighted lunges if possible.
Intermediate Australian Pull-Ups 3 sets of 8-12 reps Work on back and arm strength. Keep your body in a straight line and focus on a full range of motion.
Advanced Decline Push-Ups 4 sets of 15-25 reps Elevating your feet increases the difficulty and engages your shoulders and upper chest more.
Advanced Pistol Squats 3 sets of 5-8 reps per leg A challenging single-leg squat variation that builds strength, balance, and coordination in the legs.
Advanced Pull-Ups 4 sets of 6-10 reps Increase strength and endurance in your back, shoulders, and arms. Work towards proper form and full-range pull-ups.
Expert Handstand Push-Ups 3 sets of 5-8 reps Advanced exercise for shoulder strength and stability. Focus on building balance and strength in an inverted position.
Expert Muscle-Ups 4 sets of 3-5 reps Combination of a pull-up and a dip, targeting the chest, shoulders, and arms. Requires strength and technique to perform properly.
Expert One-Legged Squats (Pistol) 4 sets of 8-10 reps per leg Full-body strength exercise requiring significant mobility, balance, and coordination. Progress from assisted pistol squats.
Professional Planche Push-Ups 3 sets of 5-8 reps Extremely advanced push-up variation, requiring full-body strength and balance, especially in the core and shoulders.
Professional Front Lever Holds 3 sets of 10-20 seconds Engage the core and back muscles, aiming to hold the position parallel to the ground. Focus on technique and controlled movement.
Professional Human Flag 3 sets of 5-10 seconds One of the most difficult calisthenic moves requiring complete body tension and strength, especially in the core, shoulders, and obliques.
